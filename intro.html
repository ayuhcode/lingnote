What is...
Linguistics?

Have you ever wondered why languages, like English or Vietnamese, are created; or why they are so so different that we have to spend some time just to learn them? That's what Linguistics try to answer! Well actually linguistics has to answer more questions than that: the meaning (syntax), the sound (phonology), the word structure (morphology), or context of meaning (pragmatics). It can even goes beyond to other disciplines such as biolinguistics, where we study about the biological evolution of language, or psycholinguistics, which relates to psychological aspects as well.

In here, we will just learn one or two basic things about linguistics (since I don't have much expertise to go farther than that). Languages around us are so fascinating, and I hope that when you finished reading this collection, you will no longer find learning a second language boring, because there will sure be so many fun facts waiting for you!
